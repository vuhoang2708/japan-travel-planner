# Hub Analysis - Strategic Transit Points

## Tier 1: Premium Hubs (Best layover experience + pricing)

### Istanbul (IST) - Turkish Airlines Hub
- **Strengths**: Cheapest Europe connections, excellent lounge, transit hotel
- **Geography**: Equidistant Asia ↔ Europe, good for Vietnam → Europe
- **Typical savings**: 15-30% vs direct Vietnam → Europe
- **Layover tips**: Free Istanbul Tour (TK passengers, 6+ hour layover)
- **Visa**: Not required for transit (most nationalities)

### Incheon (ICN) - Korean Air / Asiana Hub
- **Strengths**: Best transit airport in Asia, free culture experiences
- **Geography**: Natural stopover Vietnam → US West Coast
- **Typical savings**: 20-35% vs direct Vietnam → US
- **Layover tips**: Free shower, transit hotel, Korean Culture Museum
- **Visa**: Transit area access without visa, KTAV for exit

### Singapore (SIN) - Singapore Airlines Hub
- **Strengths**: World-class Changi Airport, Jewel complex
- **Geography**: Perfect for Vietnam → Oceania/South Asia
- **Typical savings**: 15-25% via SIN vs direct routing
- **Layover tips**: Free city tour (5.5h+ layover), Changi Lounge
- **Visa**: 96-hour visa-free transit for most nationalities

---

## Tier 2: Value Hubs (Great for budget optimization)

### Bangkok (BKK/DMK) - AirAsia / Thai Airways
- **Strengths**: Cheapest hub to reach from Vietnam, massive LCC network
- **Risks**: Two airports (BKK for legacy, DMK for LCC) - no free transfer
- **Typical savings**: 20-40% for complex routings
- **Layover tips**: City very close to airport (30 min), cheap hotels
- **Inter-airport transfer**: ~$15 taxi, 1-1.5h, use Grab

### Kuala Lumpur (KUL) - AirAsia Hub
- **Strengths**: AirAsia's main hub, excellent LCC connections globally
- **Geography**: Good for Vietnam → Oceania, SE Asia redistribution
- **Typical savings**: 25-40% via AirAsia connections
- **Layover tips**: KLIA2 (LCC terminal) has good food court
- **Note**: KLIA1 ↔ KLIA2 transfer takes 20-30 min

### Taipei (TPE) - EVA Air / China Airlines
- **Strengths**: Good US connections, competitive prices
- **Geography**: Natural for Vietnam → US or Northeast Asia
- **Typical savings**: 15-25% vs direct
- **Layover tips**: Clean airport, reasonable food prices

---

## Tier 3: Specialist Hubs (Specific use cases)

### Doha (DOH) - Qatar Airways
- **Best for**: Premium economy value, Europe/Africa connections
- **Al Maha Services**: Excellent transit experience
- **Typical savings**: 10-20% vs direct, but higher class for same price

### Dubai (DXB) - Emirates
- **Best for**: Comfort and connectivity, Africa/Europe
- **Typical savings**: 10-15% (Emirates rarely deeply discounted)
- **Layover tips**: Free Dubai Connect hotel (10+ hour layover)

### Tokyo Narita (NRT) - ANA / JAL
- **Best for**: Vietnam → US connections via Japan
- **Typical savings**: 15-30% vs direct Vietnam → US
- **Caution**: NRT is far from city (don't plan short layover city visit)

---

## Hub Selection Quick Decision

```
Vietnam → US West:     ICN > NRT > TPE
Vietnam → US East:     ICN > IST > DOH
Vietnam → Europe:      IST > DOH > DXB
Vietnam → Oceania:     SIN > KUL > BKK
Vietnam → Japan/Korea: Direct is usually best (short-haul)
Vietnam → South Asia:  BKK > SIN > Direct
Vietnam → Africa:      IST > DOH > DXB
```
