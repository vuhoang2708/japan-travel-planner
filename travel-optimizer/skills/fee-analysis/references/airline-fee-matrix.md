## Fee Avoidance Quick Reference

| Strategy | Saves | How |
|----------|-------|-----|
| Book bags online (not counter) | 20-50% | Always pre-purchase through airline website |
| Airline credit card | $35-80/trip | First checked bag free (United Explorer, Delta Amex) |
| Status match | Bags + seats | Apply for status match between programs |
| Personal item only | $35-65 | Pack in 45×35×20cm bag (fits under seat) |
| Debit card payment | $5-15 | Avoid credit card surcharge (AirAsia, Ryanair) |
| 24-hour free cancel | Full refund | US DOT rule: free cancel within 24h of booking |
