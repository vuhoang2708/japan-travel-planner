# Pricing Patterns by Route Type

## Domestic Vietnam (VN Airlines, VietJet, Bamboo)

### Peak Seasons
| Period | Surcharge | Routes Most Affected |
|--------|-----------|---------------------|
| Tet (late Jan/Feb) | +50-100% | All routes |
| 30/4 - 1/5 | +30-50% | SGN↔HAN, leisure routes |
| Summer (Jun-Aug) | +20-40% | Beach destinations (CXR, DAD, PQC) |
| 2/9 weekend | +20-30% | All routes |
| Christmas/New Year | +15-25% | International-heavy routes |

### Sweet Spots
- **March, October, November**: Lowest demand months
- **Tuesday-Thursday flights**: 15-25% cheaper than weekend
- **Red-eye flights** (VietJet 5AM-6AM): Cheapest tier
- **Midweek round trips** (Tue→Thu): Optimal for business

### Advance Purchase
- VietJet/Bamboo: Best prices 30-45 days ahead
- Vietnam Airlines: Best prices 21-35 days ahead
- All carriers: Price spikes within 7 days of departure

## International Asia (HAN/SGN to Asia-Pacific)

### Route-Specific Patterns

**HAN/SGN → BKK/SIN/KUL** (Short-haul):
- Cheapest: Tue-Wed, 3-4 weeks ahead
- AirAsia/Scoot sales: Monthly flash deals, often Monday launches
- VietJet international: Competitive but add checked bag cost

**HAN/SGN → NRT/ICN** (Medium-haul):
- Cheapest: Oct-Nov (between summer and winter peaks)
- Cherry blossom (late Mar-Apr) and autumn leaves (Nov): +30%
- LCC option: VietJet/t'way airlines significantly cheaper than legacy

**HAN/SGN → SYD/MEL** (Long-haul in region):
- Cheapest: Feb-Mar, May (shoulder seasons)
- Peak: Dec-Jan (Southern Hemisphere summer), Jun-Jul (school holidays)
- Jetstar often has sales 60 days before travel

## Intercontinental (Vietnam ↔ US/Europe)

### Route-Specific Patterns

**Vietnam → US West Coast (SFO/LAX)**:
- Cheapest months: Feb (post-Tet), Sep-Oct, early Dec
- Most expensive: Jun-Aug (peak travel), late Dec (holidays)
- Transit hubs: ICN, NRT, TPE often cheapest
- Direct (VN Airlines HAN-SFO): Premium but saves 6-12 hours

**Vietnam → Europe (CDG/LHR/FRA)**:
- Cheapest months: Jan-Feb, Nov (excluding holidays)
- Transit via IST (Turkish): Often cheapest, 1-stop
- Transit via DOH/DXB: Competitive, good layover experience
- Peak: Jun-Aug (+30-50%)

### Day-of-Week (Intercontinental)
- Departure Tue-Wed: Consistently 10-20% cheaper
- Return Mon-Tue: Avoids weekend return premium
- Exception: Business routes (HAN-SFO) cheapest Sat departure

---

## General Pricing Rules

1. **The 54-Day Rule**: International flights are typically cheapest ~54 days before departure
2. **The Tuesday Drop**: Airlines often release sale fares on Tuesday, reflected by Wednesday
3. **The Shoulder Season**: 2-3 weeks before/after peak = best value
4. **Contra-flow**: Flying opposite to majority direction (e.g., TO Vietnam during Tet return) can be very cheap
5. **One-way vs Round-trip**: LCCs price one-way, legacy carriers sometimes discount round-trips
