**Europe:** Ryanair (FR), easyJet (U2), Wizz Air (W6), Vueling (VY), Norwegian (DY)

## Strategic Hub Routing Tips

- **Asia - US West Coast:** Check TPE, ICN (often $200-400 cheaper than direct)
- **Vietnam - Europe:** IST is frequently the cheapest connector via Turkish Airlines
- **SE Asia internal:** BKK and KUL are LCC hubs - VietJet/AirAsia connections
- **US - Europe:** Check Norwegian via LGW, Icelandair via KEF (Reykjavik)
- **Multi-city:** Kiwi virtual interlining excels at mixing LCC + FSC segments
